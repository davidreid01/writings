language

There are many forms of communication.

People learn different languages for different reasons and in different life stages.

1) Childhood language - also called your native tongue.
It is the language your parents speak and the first you learn.

2) Regional language.
The language you use to communicate to your neighbours.

3) National language.
The language used to communicate to the rest of the nation.

4) International language.
The language used to communicate to many of the human race.
English is the main one used but not the only one French, Spanish, Arabic, Mandarin (Chinese), Russian are often used, they have large audiences
and will be translated if desired.
