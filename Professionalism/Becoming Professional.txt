Becoming Professional

The first step to becoming professional is gain competency in the skills needed to deliver a useful service in exchange for money.
You cannot be 'professional' without this.

Gaining competency.
This may take 5-10 years of persistent effort.
1 Choose an industry.
	One that interests you, no one else matters in this at all.
	What is an industry?

2 Study that industry. You need to be able to answer these.
	What does it provide?
		If it provides something you morally disagree with then it is not the industry for you.
		Examples: Gambling, pornography.
	What are the jobs that industry needs to be done?

3 Gain an apprenticeship or mentorship.
