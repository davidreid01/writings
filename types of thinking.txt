Type 1:
- Fast

- Prebuilt

Type 2:
- Slow
- Creative
- Deliberate
- Analytical

Type 1 is the animalistic, quick response, shallow thinking.
Activities:
	Speaking, performance arts.

Type 2 is required to learn something new, deep thinking. Only the human animal seems capable of type 2 thinking.
Activities:
	Philosophy, science, writing.

Is Type 2 good Type 1 bad?
No.
Both types of thinking are required.
To learn something new Type 2 thinking is needed.
To become good at it Type 1 thinking is used.
Effective Type 1 is developed by practice.
